Lasagna

Description

How to make homemade lasagna like a pro.

Ingredients

  • Beef meat
  • 1 onions and 2 cloves of garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with a foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
